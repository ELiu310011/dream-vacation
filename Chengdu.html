Chengdu

A Grand City of Blossoming Diversity

As one of China's most central cities, Chengdu serves as a gathering of centuries of the country's culture and legacy. Because it is so, Chengdu has an extremely diverse religious, ethnic, and culinary atmosphere unlike any other Chinese city. When traveling here, it is essential that one samples as much of this culture as possible, by visiting its many recreational parks and restaurants.

The Sights

Similar to the rest of the Middle East, Chengdu offers a wide variety of breathtaking sites to behold, including vast mountain ranges, expansive, green plains, and the occasional blue lake. One of these scenic sites, Huanhuaxi Park, is one of many that boasts all of the previously stated facets, and offers its share of history and Chinese culture.
