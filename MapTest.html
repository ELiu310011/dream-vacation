Chengdu

As one of China's most central cities, Chengdu serves as a gathering of centuries of the country's culture and legacy. Because it is so, Chengdu has an extremely diverse religious, ethnic, and culinary atmosphere unlike any other Chinese city. When traveling here, it is essential that one samples as much of this culture as possible, by visiting its many recreational parks and restaurants.
